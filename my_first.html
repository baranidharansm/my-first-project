TITAN

Titan is a prominent Indian watch brand, founded in 1984 as a joint venture between the Tata Group and the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation. Over the decades, it has grown into a global lifestyle company with strengths in watches, jewellery, and eyewear. Titan is known for combining precision engineering with thoughtful design, offering a broad portfolio that ranges from sleek quartz watches to automatic movements, sporty chronographs, and even smartwatches. Their in-house manufacturing is highly integrated, with facilities producing millions of watches per year, which positions Titan among the world’s top watch manufacturers. Notable collections include the ultra-thin Edge, the stylish Stellar, and premium lines like Regalia — all reflecting Titan’s commitment to quality, innovation, and accessibility.
